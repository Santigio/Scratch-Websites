About Us

Who we are

Leading Edge Tech is a software based company registered in Sierra Leone

Founded in 2017, LETSL is a small web design & development agency based in Freetown, Sierra Leone. Over the last few years we've made a reputation for building websites that look great and are easy-to-use.

We aim at training the mass majority of youth and children on how to code .

We believe in giving back to humanity therefore our core value is people.

We believe in colaboration and togetherness.

At LETSL, we dream of the day when all millions of Sierra Leoneam, no matter their gender, nationality, location, or socioeconomic background, will be able to fairly participate in the global market and make their work fulfilling.
